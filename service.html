Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our painting services

How long does a typical interior painting project take?

The duration depends on the size of the space and scope of work. A standard room (12'x12') typically takes 1-2 days including preparation and cleanup. Whole house projects may take 3-7 days depending on the number of rooms and complexity.

Do I need to move all my furniture before painting?

We ask that you remove small items, wall decorations, and fragile belongings. Our team will move and cover larger furniture with protective sheeting as part of our service. We recommend removing valuables and electronics from the work area.

What type of paint do you recommend?

We use premium quality paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and Behr. The best paint depends on your surface and needs - we'll recommend the perfect sheen and formula (e.g., matte for ceilings, eggshell for walls, semi-gloss for trim) during our consultation.

How often should exterior paint be refreshed?

Most homes need exterior repainting every 5-10 years depending on climate, sun exposure, and paint quality. Wood surfaces typically require more frequent maintenance than brick or stucco. We can assess your home's condition during a free estimate.

Do you offer eco-friendly paint options?

Yes! We offer several low-VOC and zero-VOC paint options that are safer for your family and the environment. These premium paints perform just as well as conventional paints while minimizing odors and harmful emissions.
