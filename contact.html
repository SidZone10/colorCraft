Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How do I request a free estimate?

You can request a free estimate by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or emailing estimates@colorcraftmasters.com. We'll schedule a convenient time to visit your property, discuss your project, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

What areas do you service?

We serve the entire Colorville metropolitan area and surrounding communities within a 50-mile radius. This includes Downtown, Westside, East End, and all suburban neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

How long does a typical painting project take?

Project duration depends on the size and complexity. A standard room takes 1-2 days, whole interior projects 3-7 days, and exterior projects 5-10 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your estimate and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

Do I need to move all my furniture before painting?

We ask that you remove small items, wall decorations, and fragile belongings. Our team will move and cover larger furniture with protective sheeting as part of our service. We recommend removing valuables and electronics from the work area.

What type of paint do you recommend?

We use premium quality paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and Behr. The best paint depends on your surface and needs - we'll recommend the perfect sheen and formula (e.g., matte for ceilings, eggshell for walls, semi-gloss for trim) during our consultation.
